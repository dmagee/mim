About HeteroGenius Medical Image Manager

HeteroGenius Medical Image Manager (aka "MIM") is a web technology based platform for managing and processing Digital Pathology and associated Radiology Images developed by HeteroGenius Limited. MIM can run on a desktop computer (Windows/OSX/Linux), on a server, in the cloud, or even on a Linux cluster computer. The core MIM platform is free for any institution. MIM may be augmented with various paid for and free Add-on modules to perform particular image and data analysis or management tasks. MIM Pro is a fully supported paid for version of MIM which bundles a number of additional add-on features with a support contract.

HeteroGenius Limited can offer a range of bespoke customisations and additions to the MIM platform to suit customer requirements. This includes bespoke image analysis methods and workflow solutions. Additionally, we can offer OEM rebranding of MIM to clients for internal use or resale. We can work with your graphic designers, or provide our own to generate a product tailored to the functional and aesthetic requirements of your company.
